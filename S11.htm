Santa Barbara County, California (n.) 聖塔芭芭拉郡 （Santa Barbara County） 是美國加利福尼亞州南部、太平洋岸的郡。面積9,814平方公里，根據美國2010年人口普查數字，共有人口423,895人。郡治聖塔芭芭拉 （Santa Barbara）。

成立於1850年，是加州建州時的郡之一。郡名紀念天主教、東正教和西方正教會的聖人聖塔芭芭拉。

Officially the  County of Santa Barbara, is a  county  located in the  southern region  of the  U.S. state  of  California. As of the  2010 census, the population was 423,895. [3]  The  county seat  is  Santa Barbara, [5]  and the largest city is  Santa Maria.

Santa Barbara County comprises the  Santa Maria-Santa Barbara, CA  Metropolitan Statistical Area. Most of the county is part of the  California Central Coast. [6]  Mainstays of the county's economy include engineering, resource extraction (particularly petroleum extraction and diatomaceous earth mining), winemaking, agriculture, and education. The software development and tourism industries are important employers in the southern part of the county.

Southern Santa Barbara County is sometimes considered the northern cultural boundary of  Southern California. [7]
